Communication

Use this space to explain how you communicate with your team.

Teams/Slack

In my team, we primarily communicate via Slack. We also use Teams, but that is mostly reserved for discussions with people outside of the tech team, or for meetings.

Email

I personally use email for more formal messages, usually involving multiple people. I also use email to communicate with individuals outside of our organisation.

Video Call

Video calls are ideal for situations where I need to discuss an issue in detail or get immediate feedback. They allow me to ask questions and find solutions much faster. Video calls also provide some non-verbal communication cues, making conversations smoother.

Face-to-Face

Face-to-face meetings are typically reserved for collaboration and sensitive discussions. For team rituals like retrospectives or sprint planning, I prefer face-to-face communication as it feels more engaging and allows everyone to contribute more effectively.
